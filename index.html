About KAISER

KAISER is a premier national-level technical symposium that brings together the brightest minds to compete, innovate, and showcase their talents. From coding challenges to gaming arenas, we have it all.

Blind Coding

Blind Coding is a competitive programming event where your screen is turned off while you type. It tests how well you know your favorite programming language without the help of a monitor, error highlights, or auto-complete.
